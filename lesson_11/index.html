Meet & fish

Meat and fish offer a variety of health benefits due to their rich nutritional profiles. They are excellent sources of high-quality protein, which is crucial for building and repairing tissues, and also provide essential vitamins and minerals like iron, zinc, and B vitamins.

Vegetables & fruits

Fruits and vegetables offer a wide array of health benefits due to their rich content of vitamins, minerals, fiber, and antioxidants. They can help lower the risk of chronic diseases like heart disease, stroke, and certain cancers, while also promoting healthy digestion and weight management.

Sweets

In moderation, sweets can offer a few benefits like mood enhancement through endorphin release, a temporary energy boost, and potentially aiding in digestion. However, it's crucial to remember that excessive sugar consumption can lead to various health problems.

Drinks

Drinks, primarily water, offer numerous health benefits. They are essential for maintaining bodily functions, supporting physical performance, and aiding in various biological processes.
